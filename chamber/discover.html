Demographics

The city of Bountiful Utah is located in Davis County which is north of Salt Lake City. The population of Bountiful in 2024 was calculated to be 42,529. The average household income was calculated at $123,037.

Attractions

The Salt Lake Temple is located in downtown Salt Lake near the Church Office Building and the General Conference Center for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. The Salt Lake Temple is currently being fixed to make it earthquake proof. Visitors can still walk in temple square and see the temple reconstruction work.
