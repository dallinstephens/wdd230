Chamber Site Plan

Site Name

Bountiful Chamber of Commerce. Bountiful is the city in Utah which the chamber of commerce performs its functions.

Site Purpose

The Bountiful Chamber of Commerce helps provide networking for small businesses, helps create jobs for families and individuals, provides local news and the weather, and informs about local events in the area.

Scenarios

  • What is the calendar of events to facilitate business networking?
  • How do I contact the Bountiful Chamber of Commerce to get more information?
  • What is the weather forcast for this week?

Color Schema

  1. #a50000: This color may be used for the navigation/menu, h1 tag, weather section h2 background color, event button background color, and button border color for white button.
  2. #1d1a1a: This color may be used for the background color in the header and footer.
  3. #ffffff: This white color may be used for header text, logo text, event text, icon text, footer text, white button, and for some of the site background (body, etc).
  4. #c0504c: This color may be used for button background color, chamber of commerce circle background color, andn for weather background color for days of the week.
  5. #5e5555: This color may be used for block color adjacent to the event image.

Typography

  1. Lara Font (serif): This font may be used on larger text words such as h1 and h3.
  2. Open Sans Font (sans-serif): This font may be used for smaller text words such as for the p tag, menu text, button text words, etc.
